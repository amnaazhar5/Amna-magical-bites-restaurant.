Beef Korma

Beef Korma is a classic South Asian curry made with juicy beef pieces, cooked slowly in a thick, spiced yogurt-based gravy. The dish is enriched with fried onions, ginger, garlic, and aromatic whole spices, giving it a deep flavor and royal taste. It is a popular dish at weddings and family gatherings, often served with naan or steamed rice.
